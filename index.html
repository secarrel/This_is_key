This is Key! is a useful tool if you're studying GCSE's and are having trouble learning the keywords and definitions in certain subjects.

Here's how to play...

  • Select a topic you would like to practice or learn.
  • You'll see a definition and a line of underscores; this is the keyword with each letter replaced.
  • Your aim is to work out what the keyword is by using the definition and guessing one letter at a time.
  • If your guessed letter is in the keyword it will be displayed in the correct place.
  • Find all the letters before you run out of guesses to score a point!

Why not challenge a class-mate to see who can identify the most keywords?
