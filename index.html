About This is Key!

This is Key! is designed to help GCSE pupils learn and remember keywords and their definitions from topics they are studying in school. All keyword data is taken directly from the WJEC keywords glossary which is supplied to schools as a teaching aid. As a result this app is targetted at pupils in Wales who have WJEC as their exam board for most subjects.

Find more information about This is Key! on GitHub:

This is Key! GitHub repository

Here's how to play...

  • Select a topic you would like to practice or learn.
  • You'll see a definition and a line of underscores; this is the keyword with each letter replaced.
  • Your aim is to identify the keyword by using the definition and guessing one letter at a time.
  • If your guessed letter is in the keyword it will be displayed.
  • Find all the letters before you run out of guesses to score a point!

Why not challenge a class-mate to see who can identify the most keywords?
